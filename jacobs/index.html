Our Story

Four Generations.
One Promise.

It started as a neighborhood gas station. Someone needed a tire fixed, an oil changed, a car that wouldn't start. The Jacobs family showed up, did the work honestly, and charged a fair price.

That was 1928. Nearly 100 years later, nothing has changed — except the cars. Rob Jacobs, the fourth generation, has been under the hood for over 40 years. You'll find him in the back, working alongside Jason and Roger. His daughter Jenna is learning the business, ready to carry the Jacobs name into its fifth generation.

This is what it looks like when a family decides that reputation matters more than margin. One visit and you'll understand why Denver families have been coming back for generations.

Quick Answers

Common Questions

Still on the fence? Here's what people ask before their first visit.

How long have you been in business?

Since 1928 — nearly 100 years. Rob Jacobs is the fourth generation running the shop. His daughter Jenna is already learning the business for the fifth.

Are your technicians certified?

Yes. All technicians are ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certified. Every repair also comes with a 12-month / 12,000-mile warranty, whichever comes first.

Where are you and when are you open?

1310 S Parker Rd, Denver CO 80231. We're open Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Call (303) 752-1262 to schedule.

Do you charge for a diagnosis?

Diagnostics are $160/hour. We'll accurately identify the issue and walk you through what's needed before any work begins — no surprise bills.

Can you inspect a used car before I buy it?

Absolutely. $160 for a full bumper-to-bumper inspection. It's one of the smartest $160 investments you can make before committing to a used car.
